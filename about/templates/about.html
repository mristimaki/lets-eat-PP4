Welcome to Let's Eat

This recipe blog is created for my Project Portfolio 4 for my Diploma in Full Stack Software Development at Code Institute.
I chose to create a blog with content that is close to my heart.
The specific recipes along with the beautiful recipe pictures are all made by the lovely Hanna Olvenmark that has created a Swedish recipe website along with books about how to make wholesome, delicious and cheap food. You can find her website and instagram at the social media links below.

I chose to create this particular blog since I'm passionate about being out in nature as well as I love cooking and discovering new recipes to try. Connecting to nature is good for all, young and old, so by inspiring people to go out more, make a day trip or going for a longer hike, why not top the experience with some delicious food?

Thank you for looking in to this blog, and be brave, be curious about life and why we're set to be here!
